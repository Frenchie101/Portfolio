GREAT BARRIER
REEF IDENTITY

This project examines the Great Barrier Reef as both an ecological system and a site of layered cultural memory.
Beyond its natural beauty, the reef holds deep significance for First Nations communities, whose relationships to the sea, land, and dreaming are embedded within it.
